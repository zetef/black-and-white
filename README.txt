black and white [https://zetef.itch.io/black-and-white]

stage of project: prototype

you are a little heart that can change between black and white. your goal is to put the black and white tiles in the right spot. while opposite colours repel, the same colours attract, meaning you can attach to different tiles at once, or repel them from your surroundings .

controls:

move: arrow keys

action: x

exit/menu: escape
